🛏️ Accommodation strategy
San Juan base: Condado or Ocean Park. These are better for a beach-adjacent romantic trip with restaurants, parking options, and easier day-trip exits than staying directly inside Old San Juan.
Vieques base: Esperanza. This is the best fit for waterfront dinners, sunset walks, Bio Bay access, and a relaxed couples feel. Prioritize 1-bed boutique stays, guesthouses, or small romantic hotels near the malecón.
🚗 Transportation architecture
Main island: rent a car for San Juan / El Yunque / Utuado days. Drive to Ceiba, park near the ferry terminal, and ferry to Vieques as passengers.
Vieques: rent a separate Jeep/SUV on island. Do not rely on taking the San Juan rental car over. Keep departure-day transfer timing generous.

🗓️ Day 2 — Old San Juan + El Morro
San JuanIconic sightseeing
Anchor: Castillo San Felipe del Morro + Old San Juan walking loop.
Flow: hotel → El Morro → Cuartel de Ballajá → Calle Norzagaray → San Juan Cathedral → Calle del Cristo → La Fortaleza exterior → Paseo de la Princesa → Raíces Fountain → dinner.

🗓️ Day 4 — Río Tanamá gentle tubing
MountainsNature adventure
Anchor: gentle Río Tanamá cave/river tubing in Utuado.
Flow: San Juan hotel → PR-22 west → central mountain roads → Río Tanamá tour base → mountain lunch → scenic return to San Juan.

🍽️ Dining strategy
San Juan: mix Puerto Rican food, seafood, rum cocktails, and one romantic dinner. Best zones are Condado, Calle Loíza/Ocean Park, Old San Juan, and Santurce.
Luquillo / East Coast: use the Luquillo kiosks after El Yunque, or Carabalí Bar & Grill if they choose the ranch/rainforest activity branch.
Vieques: keep dinners in Esperanza so they avoid unnecessary night driving. Bio Bay night should have an early, nearby dinner.
🌦️ Mid-summer weather + backup logic
Puerto Rico will be warm, humid, and tropical. Rain can happen, and summer falls within Atlantic hurricane season, so flexible cancellation terms matter. Build the itinerary around movable outdoor anchors: if river conditions cancel tubing, swap with Old San Juan / Casa BACARDÍ / spa-rest time; if Bio Bay cancels, use Day 7 as the backup window.
